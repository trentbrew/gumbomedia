Our Story

Our Story

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Gumbo Media started as a community hub and evolved into a revolutionary content, design, and strategy agency. Recognizing a gap in the representation of Black creativity, we became a space where innovation and cultural heritage thrive. By 2020, amid societal upheavals, we reimagined our role—combining storytelling, software, and design to fuel liberation and joy.

Today, we help brands and communities tell their stories, build their products, and create impact through a combination of strategy, creative services, and technology.

Our Culture

Our Culture

Gumbo is a collective of cultural curators, digital architects, and legacy builders. We believe in the power of storytelling, design, and technology to drive social change and economic empowerment. At Gumbo, collaboration and inclusivity are at the heart of our work, as we strive to build lasting legacies and transformative solutions for the communities we serve.
